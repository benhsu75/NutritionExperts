Health starts with nutrition, and nutrition starts with
Knowledge

We believe in the power of nutrition knowledge

1) It is uniquely important

People take nutrition knowledge and apply it to their own bodies - its impact is real and physical

2) It can be powerful

Nutrition knowledge can empower people to make real changes and improve their lives

3) But, it's
broken
(and very, very much so)

Nutrition is incredibly complex, personal, and controversial

The internet is rife with misinformation, who knows what to believe?

So we started thinking...
and we wondered...

What if there was a community passionate about evidence-backed, unbiased, and relevant nutrition knowledge?

What if nutrition knowledge was disseminated by those who devote their lives to it - experts like PhDs, dietitians, and researchers?

What if learning and reading about nutrition was simple, beautiful, and easy?

What if it was democratic - what if anyone could start a discussion?
