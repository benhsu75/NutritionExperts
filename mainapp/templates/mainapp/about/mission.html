We believe in nutrition information.



It is uniquely important.

People take the nutrition information that they read online and apply it to their own bodies - it has a real and physical impact.

It is powerful.

The internet democratizes access to information, and nutrition information can empower people to make real changes and improve their lives.

But, it's broken.

Nutrition is incredibly complex, controversial, and polarizing. Online discussions devolve into pointless arguments. Experience and fact are confused. In some cases, information that is simply wrong is touted as truth.

But, Nutrition misinformation and miscommunication are not only bad, but also dangerous.

So we started to think... and we wondered

What if you could easily read and join in on the discussion about nutrition? What if there was a community of vetted experts like PhDs and dietitians? What if you could read and learn about nutrition in a feed with a simple and beautiful interface?

We're excited to bring you Nutriosity, coming very soon...
