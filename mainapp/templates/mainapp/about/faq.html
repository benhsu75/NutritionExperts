Frequently Asked Questions
What is Nutriosity?
Simply put, The Nutriosity is a community to learn about nutrition from unbiased, evidence-backed, and relevant experts. Each discussion starts with a question. Questions are answered exclusively by a community of vetted experts. Members of the community can then continue discussing the topic with experts through comments.
Can anyone ask a question?
Anyone can submit a question to be asked. As of right now (because we just launched), questions are moderated by a group of hand-selected members knowledgeable and interested in nutrition. If your question hasn’t been asked before and is a good question (what is a good question?), there is a large likelihood it will be approved.
Who is the expert community?
The expert community comprises of academics and professionals who have extensive experience in nutrition. It includes PhDs, professors, registered dietitians, researchers, and more, who have been hand-selected for their knowledge and ability to convey it.
When is this launching?
The Nutriosity is a platform for discussion about nutrition topics. The goal is to provide a community for enlightening high-quality discourse about nutrition. As such, questions that are personal in nature are likely not to get approved. That doesn’t mean you can’t ask a question about something you’ve personally encountered - it just means that each question should be applicable and relevant to more than yourself.
